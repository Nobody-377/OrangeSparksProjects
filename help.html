Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment by entering your tracking number on the Track page. You'll see real-time updates, current location, and estimated delivery time.

What do the different shipment statuses mean?

Processing: Your order is being prepared. In Transit: Your shipment is on its way. Out for Delivery: Your package will arrive today. Delivered: Your package has been successfully delivered.

How can I view my shipment history?

All your shipments are visible on the Dashboard page. You can see active and completed shipments with their current status and progress.

What if my tracking number doesn't work?

Make sure you've entered the tracking number correctly (e.g., SH-001). If the issue persists, it may take a few hours for new shipments to appear in the system.
